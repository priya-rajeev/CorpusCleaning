Linguistics Research

Now we will ask you to clean a total of ten text samples. Each sample may be anywhere from just one sentence to a few sentences long. The samples will consist of a mix of Mandarin and English words.

For each text sample, we will ask you to separate the sample into sentences, as done in the previous example.



Please carefully read the following rules:

  1. Whenever you think a sentence ends, please place double vertical lines (||) at the end of the sentence.

      For example, the following entry:

       I walked to the store today. I didn't buy anything, though.

      would become

       I walked to the store today. || I didn't buy anything, though. ||
  2. If the sentence is just a fragment, still place double vertical lines (||) at the end of the sentence.

      For example, the following entry:

       Checking out a book

      would become

       Checking out a book ||
  3. Enclose all punctuation within the double vertical lines.

      For example, the following entry:

       I am really excited about the weather today! It's so nice outside...

      would become

       I am really excited about the weather today! || It's so nice outside... ||
  4. If there is a list of steps or bullet points, segment each bullet point into its own sentence.

      For example, the following list:

      Things to do this weekend:

        grocery shop

        take a hike

        go out to dinner

      would become

       Things to do this weekend || grocery shop || take a hike || go out to dinner ||
  5. If there are any hyperlinks or email addresses, please include them in their own sentence (there should be a set of vertical bars before and after the link/email). Do this even if doing so will break up a sentence.

      For example, the following entry:

       For concerns about the job posting at google.com, please email the recruiter at recruiter@recruiter.com - thanks!

      would become

       For concerns about the job posting at || google.com ||, please email the recruiter at || recruiter@recruiter.com || - thanks!||
  6. If there are any timestamps, please include them in their own sentence (there should be a set of vertical bars before and after the timestamp). Do this even if doing so will break up a sentence.

      For example, the following entry:

       I received a scam call at (2020-05-23 20:43 +8:00), which is an odd time to call.

      would become

       I received a scam call at || (2020-05-23 20:43 +8:00) ||, which is an odd time to call.
  7. If there are any emojis or emoticons, try to include them in whichever sentence seems closest to you.

      For example, the following entry:

       I'm so happy :) I ate some pancakes today!

      could become

       I'm so happy :) || I ate some pancakes today! ||
  8. All in all, use your best judgement! There are no wrong or right answers :)
