Linguistics Research

Now we will ask you to clean a total of ten text samples. Each sample may be anywhere from just one sentence to a few sentences long. The samples will consist of a mix of Mandarin and English words.

For each text sample, we will ask you to separate the sample into sentences, as done in the previous example.

Use the following rules:

  1. Whenever you think a sentence ends, please place a double forward slash (\\) at the end of the sentence.
  2. If the sentence is just a fragment, still place a double forward slash (\\) at the end of the sentence.
  3. Enclose all punctuation within the forward slashes. For example, if the sentence ends with an exclamation, place the double forward slash after the exclamation mark.
  4. If there is a list of steps or bullet points, segment each bullet point within its own sentence.
  5. If there are any hyperlinks, please remove them.
